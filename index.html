STRIKERS Elite Sports Group

STRIKERS Elite Sports Group is a specialist sports medicine and performance science group based in Dublin, Ireland, working in collaboration with the School of Medicine, University of St Andrews. Our academic programme focuses on integrated health and performance systems across elite sport, military, and high-demand human environments.
